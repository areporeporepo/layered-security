# Escalation: The Right Channel Per Layer

Each layer has a channel that can actually act. The log + the score is what you bring to it.
Bring the **factual** column and the evidence refs, not the inference column.

> Verify current names/links yourself — organizations and URLs change. This is a starting map, not legal advice.

## Workplace
1. **Internal:** HR / ombuds, in writing, referencing your employer's written policy.
2. **External (US):** EEOC (`eeoc.gov`) for discrimination/retaliation tied to a protected
   class; state fair-employment agency (e.g. CA Civil Rights Dept) often has stronger rules.
3. **Lawyer:** an employment attorney — many do free initial consults and work on contingency.
   Bring your timeline; it's most of their work, done.

## Social / Online
1. Platform reporting + block; preserve screenshots with URLs/timestamps **before** blocking.
2. For threats, doxxing, impersonation: the platform's legal/safety team, and police if
   there's a credible threat.

## Neighborhood / Public
1. Non-emergency police line to **create a record** of a pattern (not for one cold stare —
   for a documented, repeating, escalating thing).
2. If a landlord/HOA is involved, written notice to them, dated.

## Home / Physical — highest priority
1. **Police report every time.** The pattern lives in the stack of report numbers.
2. Locksmith / re-key; entry sensors + a door camera so claims become footage.
3. If reports go nowhere: the police department's oversight/internal-affairs or a civilian
   review board, and a lawyer.

## Institutional failure (a channel that won't act)
1. The body above it: a state agency over a local one, a licensing board, an inspector general.
2. A lawyer (the non-response is now part of your documented record).
3. Local journalists cover documented, dated patterns — emphasis on *documented*.

## When in doubt about safety
- **Immediate danger:** emergency services (911 in the US).
- **Crisis / overwhelmed:** in the US, call or text **988** (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline). It's
  not only for suicide — it's for anyone carrying more than they can hold alone. Using it is
  the same move as everything else in this repo: getting a situation in front of someone who
  can act on it.

---

### The pattern across all of these
Find the channel with **power to act**, hand it a **dated factual record**, and if it won't
act, **escalate to the body above it** — logging each non-response as you go. That loop is the
whole game.
